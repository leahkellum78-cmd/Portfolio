About

A storyteller working with light, sound, and timing.

I'm a video editor and VFX artist, studyied at Texas Tech University. I work mostly in Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects on commercial, news, and short-form story projects — anywhere the cut and the visual effects do as much storytelling as the words.

Outside of post-production I shoot photography and design websites, which keeps my eye for composition sharp.
